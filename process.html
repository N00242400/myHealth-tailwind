Research

Secondary Research

We started by looking at existing apps and information about how older adults use technology. This helped us understand common problems they face and what kinds of features work well. It gave us a good foundation and helped us see what our app needed to do.

Target audeince

By studying how older adults use technology, we identified their need for clarity, larger text, and simple navigation. These insights guided us in creating an app that feels supportive and easy to use for them and their carers.

  • • Higher risk of depression around 14% of adults aged 60+ live with a mental health condition.
  • • Increased likelihood of high blood pressure and cardiovascular issues.
  • • Poor sleep can raise blood pressure and negatively affect wellbeing.
  • • Many older adults struggle to keep track of daily medication schedules.
  • • Need for large text, clarity, and simple navigation to support accessibility.
  • • Integrated features like mood tracking, cognitive exercises, and reminders can improve overall wellbeing.
