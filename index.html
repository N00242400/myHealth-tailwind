With myHealth you can track…

Mood & Wellness

Log how you feel each day, track your energy levels, and spot patterns between mood, stress and lifestyle. Use simple daily check-ins to build better habits and share insights with your care team if you want to.

Medication

Keep all your pills, doses, and schedules in one place with clear reminders so you never miss a dose. Mark medication as taken in a tap and review your adherence over days or weeks for extra peace of mind.

Heart Health

Record heart rate and blood pressure readings and see how they change over time in clear, simple charts. Highlight trends, save important measurements, and bring a clean history to your next appointment.

Activity

Track your daily movement, from steps and walks to workouts, and stay motivated to move a little more. Set realistic goals, celebrate streaks, and see how activity supports your overall health and mood.

Sleep Insights

Log bedtimes, wake-ups, and sleep quality to understand what helps you rest better each night. Spot patterns in your routine, experiment with small changes, and track how they affect your sleep.
